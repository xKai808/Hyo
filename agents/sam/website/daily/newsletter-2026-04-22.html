## Also Moving · Privacy vs. Progress

Meta has stirred the pot with its latest policy to monitor employee mouse movements and keystrokes. The data collected will be used to train AI models, aiming to improve AI's understanding of human-computer interaction. While this move could lead to significant advancements in AI, it raises serious privacy concerns among employees and privacy advocates. The balance between technological progress and individual privacy rights is once again in the spotlight.

Meanwhile, OpenAI has released ChatGPT Images 2.0, an AI model that combines image generation with reasoning capabilities. This advancement allows for more contextually accurate and relevant image outputs, enhancing user interaction and satisfaction. As AI continues to evolve, the integration of reasoning with generation marks a significant step forward.
