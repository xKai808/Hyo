The Lab · What AI can newly do this week

A recent study titled "The Tool-Overuse Illusion" explores why large language models (LLMs) tend to prefer external tools over their internal knowledge. This phenomenon, termed tool overuse, reveals a tendency in LLMs to rely excessively on external tools, which can impact their reasoning efficiency. Understanding this behavior is crucial for optimizing AI systems to enhance their internal reasoning capabilities.
