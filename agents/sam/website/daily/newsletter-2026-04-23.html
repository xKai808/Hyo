Tool-Overuse Illusion

A recent study titled "The Tool-Overuse Illusion" explores why large language models (LLMs) tend to prefer external tools over their internal knowledge. This phenomenon, known as tool overuse, can lead to inefficiencies in AI systems. The study highlights the importance of balancing tool integration with internal reasoning capabilities to optimize AI performance.
