# Lab: End-to-End Autonomous Scientific Discovery

## First seen
2026-04-30 · Ra brief

## What it is
An LLM-based agent that ran a complete scientific experiment on real optical hardware — hypothesis formation, experimental design, physical execution, revision — without human intervention

## Why it's interesting
First documented nontrivial research result produced by AI on physical hardware in a closed loop; closes the simulation-vs-reality gap

## When we'd reach for it
Any domain where experiments are cheap but hypothesis space is large: materials science, drug discovery, process optimization
