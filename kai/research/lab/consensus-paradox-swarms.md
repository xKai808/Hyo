# Lab: The Consensus Paradox in Agent Swarms

## First seen
2026-04-30 · Ra brief

## What it is
Multi-agent AI systems that share the same architecture tend to prioritize internal agreement over external accuracy

## Why it's interesting
36-experiment study across 12,804 trajectories shows homogeneous swarms converge confidently on wrong answers — groupthink at machine speed, with role fidelity failure as a key mechanism

## When we'd reach for it
Critical design constraint for any multi-agent review, adversarial checking, or verification pipeline — architectural diversity is required for genuine independence
