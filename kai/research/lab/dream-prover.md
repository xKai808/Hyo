# Lab: DreamProver — Wake-Sleep Theorem Proving

## First seen
2026-04-30 · Ra brief

## What it is
Agent that proves theorems in a 'wake' phase, then abstracts reusable sub-proofs into a growing library during a 'sleep' phase — making each future proof cheaper

## Why it's interesting
Shifts formal reasoning from one-off generation to cumulative knowledge; the library compounds across sessions instead of resetting each time

## When we'd reach for it
Formal verification of smart contracts, math-heavy reasoning pipelines, any domain where correctness guarantees matter more than speed of iteration
