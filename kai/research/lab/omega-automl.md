# Lab: OMEGA: Auto-AI Research Framework

## First seen
2026-04-30 · Ra brief

## What it is
End-to-end system that generates novel ML algorithms autonomously — from hypothesis to executable code — and verifies they outperform baselines

## Why it's interesting
Closes the loop that was previously human-in-the-loop: the judgment call about whether an experiment result is worth pursuing. OMEGA does that automatically.

## When we'd reach for it
Benchmarking new model architectures, automating ablation studies, exploring large spaces of algorithm variants before committing engineering time to a hand-designed solution
