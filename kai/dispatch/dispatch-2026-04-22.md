**On keeping SICQ alongside OMP:** exactly the right call. SICQ catching a broken process and OMP catching a process that ran without producing anything are different failure modes. An agent that drops on SICQ while OMP is fine means the cycle is running less often but still producing results when it does. Different diagnoses, different responses.

**The five umbrella metrics** come from the CLEAR framework (enterprise agentic AI evaluation, arxiv 2511.14136), SRE practices, and the SAP agent-quality-inspect library.
